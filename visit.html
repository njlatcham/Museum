Hours and Admission

Opening Hours

Open Daily 9:30-6:00, Monday until 8:00

Museum will be closed Thanksgiving, Christmas and New Years Day.

Admission Prices

  • Adults $25
  • Seniors (65+) $10
  • Students (12 and up) $10
  • Members Free
  • Children (12 and under) Free

Visitors can park for free in the caridonm to the Museum

Directions

The Museum of Art and History is located in the awesome Snow Road neighbourhood, just steps from the Sachan Garden and 2 minutes from downtown London
